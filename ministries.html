Our Ministries

At Tokyo International SDA Church, we believe in serving our community through various ministries that meet spiritual, physical, and social needs. Each ministry is designed to help people grow in their relationship with God and with one another.

Media Ministry

Media Ministry

Our media ministry uses technology to spread God's message through live streaming, social media, podcasts, and video content. We aim to reach both local and global audiences with inspiring messages.

  • Live Streaming: Every Sabbath service
  • Social Media: Daily devotionals and updates
  • Video Production: Sermons and testimonies
  • Photography: Capturing church events
Youth Ministry

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry provides a supportive environment where young people can explore their faith, build lasting friendships, and develop leadership skills while serving their community.

  • Youth Sabbath School: Saturday at 9:30 AM
  • AY Society: Saturday evening programs
  • Youth Fellowship: Monthly social activities
  • Mission Trips: Annual service opportunities
Children's Ministry

Children's Ministry

Our children's ministry provides a safe, loving environment where children can learn about God's love through Bible stories, crafts, music, and interactive activities tailored to their developmental stage.

  • Sabbath School: Every Saturday at 9:30 AM
  • Children's Church: Age-appropriate worship
  • Vacation Bible School: Summer program
  • Story Time: Weekly Bible story sessions
Pathfinders

Pathfinders

Pathfinders is a worldwide organization sponsored by the Seventh-day Adventist Church, designed for children ages 10-15. It offers action, adventure, challenge, and group activities that produce team spirit and loyalty to God.

  • Weekly Meetings: Every Saturday afternoon
  • Camping Trips: Quarterly outdoor adventures
  • Honor Classes: Over 200 skill-building activities
  • Community Service: Regular outreach projects
Adventurers

Adventurers

The Adventurer Club is a program for children ages 6-9, designed to strengthen parent-child relationships by providing specialized weekly activities tuned into the psychological needs of this age group.

  • Weekly Meetings: Every Saturday afternoon
  • Bible Stories: Interactive learning experiences
  • Nature Activities: Outdoor exploration and crafts
  • Family Events: Parent-child bonding activities
Music Ministry

Music Ministry

Music is an integral part of our worship experience. Our music ministry includes various groups and opportunities for musicians and vocalists of all skill levels to use their talents in service to God.

  • Choir: Rehearsals Friday evenings
  • Worship Team: Contemporary praise and worship
  • Special Music: Solo and ensemble performances
  • Instrumental Groups: Strings, brass, and percussion
Health Ministry

Health Ministry

Our health ministry follows the biblical principle that our bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit. We provide education and programs that promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

  • Health Seminars: Quarterly educational programs
  • Cooking Classes: Plant-based nutrition
  • Health Screenings: Regular check-up events
  • Exercise Groups: Walking and fitness programs
